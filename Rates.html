What is Low Income Leniency?

Provide visual, in-person proof of the household's tax return or unemployment to qualify into reduced rates. 

The qualifications follow the Federal Poverty Level- Click here to see the .gov info page

  • $5/hr rate reduction for households within 200% of the federal poverty level
  • $10/hr rate reduction for households within the federal poverty level

Unexpected, strenuous financial situations can be hard to prove, but we are more than willing to work something out

What is Net-Zero Initiative?

The greater Lansing area has been able to donate a collection of protected, underutilized, and structurally-sound moving boxes for the community and environment to benefit from, on very simple conditions-

  • Borrow up to 5 boxes for free (will hopefully increase with the community's continued support)
  • Give verbal intent to return (or more!) after unpacking

That's it! No pressure to monitor, as it is simply a community driven and highly praised initiative to make no box go underutilized or pile up in a nice, moved-in home.

AND you also have the option to borrow our clear 70 qt storage tubs via a fully refundable deposit!

  • $5 per tub short term option (1 week)
  • $10 per tub long term option (2 weeks+)

To return boxes and/or tubs, come to us (most appreciated) or have us swing by for pick up
